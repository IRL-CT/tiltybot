Ready

1. Release motors

Turn off torque so you can move the robot by hand.

2. Position the robot

Move it to the home position: head level, looking straight ahead.

3. Set home + limits

Writes homing offset (current = center) and position limits to EEPROM. Persists across power cycles.

4. Test

Move to center, min, max to verify.

Reset to defaults

Clear homing offset, restore full range (0-4095).
